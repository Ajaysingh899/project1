lathe machine

A lathe machine is a versatile tool used for shaping materials like metal, wood, or plastic. It operates by rotating the workpiece against a cutting tool, which can be adjusted for various angles and depths. The primary functions include turning, drilling, and threading, making it crucial in manufacturing and woodworking. Lathe machines come in different types, such as engine lathes, turret lathes, and CNC lathes, each offering specific features for precision and automation.
